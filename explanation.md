# Explanation of Work
### about 

A visual recording of the third-space wonder that is the Denny's in Leominster, MA.

### aesthetic intent

Using my laptop's microphone, I captured live audio of the bustling sounds of a late-night visit to Denny's. By live-commenting in and out lines of code, either on the beat
of the overhead music or when the moment felt right, I shifted the animation through a miniature narrative of what it's like for me to experience this communal 
third-space that I've come to associate with my creative scholarship over the course of the last of graduate work.

While there is a planned narrative for the animation, once that was completed the livecoding moved from ordered to experimental, playing with the colors and options available 
without concern for a clean finish. This reflects the open-ended existence of the 24-hour Denny's; a self-contained world that doesn't artificially close out one's 
experience within it, but instead provides time, space, and support to be try something, maybe fail (but maybe not!)--and still have a seat at the table.
